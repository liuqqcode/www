Pit and fissure sealant, in simple terms, is to make a protective film on the surface of the child's teeth, reduce the chance of tooth decay, and reduce the rate of dental caries in children. Its importance is self-evident, can reduce children's dental children bring pain, reduce the chance of children's bad teeth.
