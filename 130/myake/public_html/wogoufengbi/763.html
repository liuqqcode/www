    做窝沟封闭的较佳时间是什么时候？萌出后儿童牙齿达到咬合平面，即适合窝沟封闭，一般在4年内萌出。其他人的牙齿，尤其是患龋或有龋齿倾向的患侧牙，应采用窝沟封闭。

When is the best time to do a pit and fissure closure?

做窝沟封闭的较佳时间是什么时候？

The best time for occlusion is: deciduous molars are 3-4 years old, first molars are 6-7 years old, second molars are 11-13, and bicuspid teeth are 9-13 years old. Disabled children with poor oral hygiene may consider relaxing the age of the pit and fissure, although they are older or have longer mouth eruption.

    治疗的时间是：乳磨牙3-4岁，恒磨牙在6-7岁，第二磨牙和双尖牙牙11-13，9-13岁。口腔卫生不良的残疾儿童可考虑放宽窝沟的年龄。

Nest ditch closed method is very simple, through cleaning teeth, etching, washing, drying and curing sealant coating steps can be completed. After curing, the material is tightly bonded with the groove wall, and has certain anti chewing pressure, which is not harmful to eating, and the material is non-toxic after curing and harmless to the human body. But it needs to be emphasized that it is necessary for professionals to operate the pit and fissure sealing system, and the necessary instruments and equipment are needed. Sign of success is the fissure sealant can exist, but not suspicious wear off, so the need for regular inspection, if the material to be re closed off.
