    在父母的眼中都是特别重要的。但仍有不少孩子有畸形牙的症状，这是由于吃手指、咬东西、爱吐舌头等不良习惯引起的。所以，对于这些孩子来说，如何防止牙齿问题？

How do you prevent dental problems in children?

如何预防儿童牙齿的问题？

rú如 hé何 yù预 fáng防 ér儿 tóng童 yá牙 chǐ齿 de的 wèn问 tí题 ？

1, correct bad habits. Let children eat sweets, found eating fingers, lips, tongue, breathing and other bad habits, parents must guide timely reminder, patient education, help children overcome bad habits.

1，纠正坏习惯。让孩子吃甜食，发现吃手指、嘴唇、舌头、呼吸等不良习惯，家长要及时引导提醒，耐心教育，帮助孩子克服不良习惯。

2, pay attention to oral health. Strengthen nutrition and pay attention to oral hygiene, found dental caries, timely filling dental caries.

2，注意口腔卫生。加强营养，注意口腔卫生，发现龋齿，及时充填龋齿。

3, reasonable feeding. Promote breastfeeding and avoid bottle caries. The use of artificial feeding, it is best to use approximate nipple nipple, nipple hole can not be too large, diameter 1-2 millimeter advisable; children to chew food, should eat a little stiff and easy to digest food to exercise the muscles of mastication, promote the masticatory organ development.

3，合理喂养。提倡母乳喂养，避免奶瓶龋。采用人工喂养，使用近似乳头，乳头孔不能太大，直径1-2毫米为宜；孩子咀嚼食物，应多吃一点僵硬和易消化的食物以锻炼咀嚼肌，促进咀嚼器官的发育。
