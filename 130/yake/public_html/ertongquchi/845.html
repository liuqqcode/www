Why does a drink hurt your teeth?

为什么喝酒会伤害你的牙齿？

wèi为 shén什 me么 hē喝 jiǔ酒 huì会 shāng伤 hài害 nǐ你 de的 yá牙 chǐ齿 ？

The acid in the drink corrodes the teeth, which can occur immediately after contact between the drink and the tooth. The sugar in the beverage is fermented by microorganisms in the plaque and continues to produce acid. This change takes several minutes to an hour. Children who drink sweet and sour drinks at night and don't brush their teeth have the highest rates of caries. As long as the drinking time of sweet and sour drinks is prolonged, the harm of acid etching can be increased regardless of what kind of drinking is used (such as sucking or drinking with a cup).

    饮料中的酸腐蚀牙齿，可饮料和牙齿之间接触后立即发生。饮料中的糖由菌斑中的微生物发酵，并继续产生酸。这个变化需要几分钟到一小时。晚上喝甜饮料和不刷牙的孩子患龋率高。只要延长酸味饮料的饮用时间，无论使用何种饮料（如用杯子吸或喝），酸蚀的危害都会增加。
