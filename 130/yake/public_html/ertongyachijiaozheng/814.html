    儿童牙齿护理是否安全，许多家长都担心儿童的牙齿畸形，导致儿童牙齿畸形的因素很多。常见的畸形牙拥挤牙齿，龅牙，牙列稀疏，地包天等。儿童牙齿畸形需要及时纠正。但家长们也担心孩子的口腔正畸安全，那么儿童牙齿矫正安全吗？

Dental care is safe for children, and experts say it is unnecessary to worry about whether children's teeth can cause harm. Study of modern orthodontics: orthodontic force induced by light tooth movement, is a biological conversion process is slow, moving side of tooth alveolar bone absorption, on the other side of the alveolar bone regeneration and reconstruction, the correction of the tooth after the re positioning of long stability. Therefore, regular orthodontic treatment will not cause trauma to the teeth and periodontal tissues.
